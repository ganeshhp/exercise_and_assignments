## creating Job API object

apiVersion: batch/v1
kind: Job
metadata:
  name: test-job
  namespace: webapp
spec:
  completions: 5
  parallelism: 1
  backoffLimit: 4
  template:
    spec:
      restartPolicy: Never
      containers:
      - name: container-job
        image: alpine:latest
        command: ["echo", "Hello World"]
